Frequently Asked Questions

Is my password safe when I use your checker?

Yes, absolutely. The password checker is a **100% client-side tool**. All analysis is done in your web browser using JavaScript. The password you type never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Does the password generator save my passwords?

No. The secure password generator creates unique passwords on your device in real time. We do not store, save, or have access to any passwords you generate. The process is completely private.

How does your tool know if my password is strong?

The tool checks your password against established security rules, such as length and the inclusion of uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. It also compares against a small list of extremely common passwords to warn you if yours is too easy to guess. All of this happens offline, on your own computer.

Can I use the tools on my phone or tablet?

Yes. Password Health Checker works on any modern browser, whether you’re using a desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphone.

Is it really free to use?

Yes. Password Health Checker is completely free, with no registration or hidden costs.
